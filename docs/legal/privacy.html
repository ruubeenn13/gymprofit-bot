Privacy Policy

What data GymProBot processes, why, and what rights you have over it.

Last updated: 23 July 2026

This policy explains what data GymProBot (the “Bot”), a Discord bot linked to the GymProFit community, processes, for what purpose, and what rights you have over it. By adding the Bot to a server or using its commands, you accept this processing.

01Data controller

The data controller is Rubén Juan Candela, the Bot’s developer and owner (the “Controller”). Contact for any privacy matter: gymprofit.soporte@gmail.com.

The Bot is operated from Spain; processing is governed by Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and Spanish data-protection law (LOPDGDD).

02What data we process

The Bot processes only the minimum data needed to work:

DataPurpose
Discord user ID (numeric) and username Link your progress (level, economy, RPG) to your account and show it in commands and rankings.
Server (guild) ID and channel IDs Configure the Bot per server and post messages in the right channels.
Gameplay data you generate: experience (XP), level, in-game currency, RPG stats, streaks, inventory and challenge progress Provide the community’s gamification and economy features.
Moderation metadata (warnings and staff action logs), when the server uses those features Community moderation and traceability of staff actions.
Linked GymProFit account, if you choose to use the linking command Show your GymProFit app progress inside Discord (only after a voluntary action by you).

The Bot does not permanently store the content of your messages. When it processes a message (e.g. for auto-moderation or commands) it does so in the moment and does not keep it as history, other than the strictly necessary metadata described above.

03Legal basis

  • Performance of the service you request by using the Bot and its commands.
  • Legitimate interest of the Controller and the community in keeping a functional, moderated and abuse-free server.
  • Consent for optional features, such as linking your GymProFit account, which is only enabled by an action of yours.

04Where data is stored

Data is stored in a managed database hosted within the European Union. Access is restricted to the Controller. The GymProFit app and its API, which the Bot communicates with for linking features, have their own privacy policy.

05Who we share it with

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes. Only the providers strictly needed to run the service are involved (Discord as the platform, the database provider and, if you use linking, the GymProFit API), acting as data processors.

06Retention

We keep your data while you use the Bot or belong to a server where it is installed. You can request deletion at any time (see the next section). When you delete your account, the gameplay data linked to your Discord ID is removed.

07Your rights

You have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict and object to the processing of your data, as well as data portability. The Bot provides commands to view and delete your own gameplay data; for any other request, email gymprofit.soporte@gmail.com. You may also lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (aepd.es).

08Minors

The Bot is intended for users who meet the minimum age required by Discord’s Terms of Service. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

09Changes to this policy

We may update this policy; the “Last updated” date reflects the current version. Significant changes will be announced through the community’s usual channels.
